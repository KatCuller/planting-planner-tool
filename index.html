What is this tool?

This tool helps you find the best planting time for vegetables and flowers based on your USDA hardiness zone.

How to use this Tool?

Select your planting zone and then choose either a vegetable or a flower to receive the optimal planting time.

FAQs

  • Q: What if I don't know my planting zone? A: You can find your planting zone on the map above.
  • Q: Can I use this tool for both vegetables and flowers? A: Yes, you can choose either vegetables or flowers, but not both at the same time.
